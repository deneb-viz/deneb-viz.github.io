Getting started
Examples & resources

Everything included

Deneb contains all libraries needed for your visual to work anywhere your Power BI report does, including publish to web, on-premise with Report Server, and mobile.

Customizable *and* actionable

In addition to the expressiveness of the Vega languages, Deneb can integrate with Power BI's interactivity features, including tooltips, cross-filtering and drill-through.

Microsoft certified

Deneb is certified by Microsoft, meaning that it doesn't access external services or resources and can be exported to PDF or displayed in emails.

Free and open source

Deneb is released under the MIT License, and is free of charge.
